Stir-fried Beef and Egg with Oyster - Bento Menu

Posted on April 9, 2019
viewed

Ingredients ( 1 serve )
beef slice 80 g
1 egg
soy 1/2 tsp
sugar 1/2 tsp
oyster 2 tsp
oil,shallot,pepper,sesame

Instructions

  1. Cut the beef into easy to eat lengths. Beat the eggs into the container, add salt and pepper, and mix well.

  2. Pour the salad oil into the frying pan and open the fire. Pour in the egg mixture and stir it up.

  3. Add the beef in an empty frying pan, fry until it is discolored, and season with sugar, soy and oyster. Put egg in, add chopped green onions and sesame seeds, cut into small pieces, mix well and turn off the heat.

You can add your favorite green vegetables and the colors will be more beautiful.
